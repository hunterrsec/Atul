Our Story

A Family-Run Paradise
by the Sea

Kamaxi Beach Resort & Yoga Retreat is a beachfront property nestled on the pristine shores of Polem Beach, Canacona, South Goa. Founded by Mr. Laxman Raikar and now lovingly managed by his son Mr. Atul Laxman Raikar, we blend authentic Goan warmth with modern comforts.

Whether you seek the rustic charm of our wooden sea-facing huts, the luxury of our deluxe AC rooms, or the magic of waking up to ocean views — we have something extraordinary for every traveller.

Our Legacy

The Kamaxi Story

Two generations of passion, stewardship, and authentic Goan hospitality by the sea.

The Raikar Family

Founders & Guardians of Kamaxi

The Foundation of a Dream

Kamaxi Beach Resort began with a singular, heartfelt vision by its founder, Mr. Laxman Raikar. Driven by a deep love for the serene Goan coastline and a desire to share the authentic beauty of these shores with the world, he laid the foundation of what would become a sanctuary for travellers from across India and beyond.

He didn't just build accommodation — he established a philosophy of hospitality rooted in warmth, humility, and the utmost respect for the land. Every decision was guided by a deep reverence for the natural beauty of Polem Beach and the rich traditions of Goan culture.

A New Generation of Stewardship

As the resort flourished, so did the involvement of his son, Mr. Atul Laxman Raikar. Taking the helm with fresh energy and an even deeper sense of responsibility, Atul built upon his father's legacy while evolving Kamaxi into a more complete retreat experience. Under his stewardship, the resort became not just a place to stay, but a dedicated guardian of this precious stretch of coastline.

Cultivating Natural Beauty

Atul's passion extends far beyond the walls of the resort. He recognised early on that the soul of Kamaxi lies in the pristine sands and the rhythmic tides of Polem Beach. He personally spearheaded initiatives to protect and enhance the beachfront environment:

Shoreline Purity

Rigorous daily cleaning standards to keep Polem's sands spotless and welcoming for all.

Nature First

Preserving the natural flora, fauna, and ecosystems that make this location truly unique.

Sustainable Growth

Growing resort amenities thoughtfully while maintaining the untouched feel of the landscape.

Our Promise to You

Today, Kamaxi Beach Resort stands as a testament to two generations of the Raikar family's dedication. When you stay with us, you aren't just a guest at our resort — you become part of a legacy that values the environment as much as your personal comfort. We invite you to experience the natural beauty that the Raikar family has spent decades nurturing and protecting. Welcome to our home. Welcome to Kamaxi.

Getting Here

Directions & Taxi Services

Polem Beach, Canacona, South Goa — We'll help you arrive comfortably and hassle-free.

How to Reach Us

FromGoa International Airport (Dabolim)

Take NH66 South via Margao towards Chaudi / Canacona. Continue to Loliem and follow signs to Polem Beach. The resort is on the beachfront.

~75 km ~1.5 – 2 hrs by road
FromCanacona Railway Station (Chaudi)

Canacona (Chaudi) is the nearest railway station on the Konkan Railway line. From there, take a taxi or auto-rickshaw directly to Polem Beach — a short, easy ride.

~8 km ~15 – 20 min
FromMargao / Panaji by Bus

Take a state or private bus from Margao Bus Stand to Canacona (Chaudi). From Chaudi bus stand, a local taxi or auto-rickshaw will take you directly to Polem Beach.

~60 km from Margao ~1.5 hrs by bus
FromMopa Airport (North Goa)

From the new Mopa Airport in North Goa, take NH66 South all the way to Canacona. Continue to Loliem / Polem Village and follow signs to Polem Beach.

~140 km ~2.5 – 3 hrs by road

Travel Tips & Advice

Pre-book your airport taxi well in advance, especially during peak season (November – February). Demand is very high and last-minute bookings can be difficult or expensive.

Goa Miles is the most reliable and transparent option, with government-set fixed fares and no surge pricing — our top recommendation for the airport-to-Polem journey.

Polem Beach is at the southernmost tip of Goa, near the Karnataka border. Always tell your driver: "Kamaxi Beach Resort, Polem Beach, Loliem, Canacona."

WhatsApp our team before you depart and we'll send you a Google Maps pin to share with your driver — ensuring a smooth, stress-free arrival.
