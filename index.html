Our Story

A Family-Run Paradise
by the Sea

Kamaxi Beach Resort is a beachfront property nestled at the pristine Polem Beach in Canacona, South Goa. As a family-run retreat, we blend warm Goan hospitality with modern comforts to create an unforgettable escape.

Whether you're seeking the rustic charm of our wooden cottages, the luxury of our deluxe AC rooms, or the magic of waking up to sea views — we have something extraordinary for every traveller.
