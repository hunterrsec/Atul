Our Story

A Family-Run Paradise

Whether you're seeking the rustic charm of our wooden cottages, the luxury of our deluxe AC rooms, or the magic of waking up to sea views — we have something extraordinary for every traveller.

Our Legacy

The Kamaxi Story

Two generations of passion, stewardship, and Goan hospitality

The Raikar Family

Founders & Guardians of Kamaxi

The Foundation of a Dream

Kamaxi Beach Resort began with a singular vision by its founder, Mr. Laxman Raikar. Driven by a love for the serene coastline and a desire to share the authentic beauty of our shores with the world, he laid the first stones of what would become a sanctuary for travelers.

He didn't just build a restaurant & bar — he established a philosophy of hospitality rooted in warmth and respect for the land. Every decision was guided by a deep reverence for the natural beauty of Polem Beach and the traditions of Goan culture.

A New Generation of Stewardship

As the resort grew, so did the involvement of his son, Mr. Atul Laxman Raikar. Taking the helm at a remarkably young age, Atul stepped into his father's footsteps with fresh energy and a deep sense of responsibility. Under his oversight, Kamaxi Beach Resort evolved from a destination into a dedicated guardian of the coast.

Cultivating Natural Beauty

Atul's passion went beyond the walls of the resort. He recognized that the soul of Kamaxi lies in the pristine sands and the rhythmic tides of the beach. He personally spearheaded initiatives to develop and protect the beachfront:

Shoreline Purity

Implementing rigorous daily cleaning standards to keep our sands spotless.

Nature Honored

Preserving the natural flora and fauna that make this location unique.

Sustainable Growth

Growing resort amenities while maintaining the untouched feel of the landscape.

Our Promise to You

Today, Kamaxi Beach Resort stands as a testament to two generations of the Raikar family. When you stay with us, you aren't just a guest at our resort — you are part of a legacy that values the environment as much as your comfort. We invite you to experience the natural beauty that the Raikar family has spent decades protecting. Welcome to our home. Welcome to Kamaxi.

Getting Here

Directions & Taxi Services

Polem Beach, Canacona, South Goa — We'll help you get here comfortably

How to Reach Us

From
Goa International Airport (Dabolim)

Take NH66 South towards Margao, then continue to Chaudi/Canacona. Turn towards Polem Beach. The resort is on the beachfront.

~75 km~1.5 – 2 hrs
From
Canacona Railway Station

Canacona (Chaudi) is the nearest railway station on the Konkan Railway line. From there, take a taxi or auto-rickshaw to Polem Beach — about 8 km away.

~8 km~15–20 min
From
Margao / Panaji (Bus)

Take a state or private bus from Margao Bus Stand to Canacona (Chaudi). From Chaudi, a local taxi or auto will take you to Polem Beach.

~60 km from Margao~1.5 hrs
From
Mopa Airport (Sindhudurg)

From the new Mopa Airport in North Goa, take NH66 South all the way to Canacona. Continue to Polem Village and follow signs to Polem Beach.

~140 km~2.5 – 3 hrs

Travel Tips

Pre-book your airport taxi, especially during peak season (November–February). Last-minute bookings can be difficult to arrange.

Goa Miles is the most reliable and transparent option with fixed government-set fares — great for the airport-to-Polem journey.

Polem Beach is at the southernmost tip of Goa — near the Karnataka border. Inform your driver of the exact location: "Kamaxi Beach Resort, Polem Beach, Loliem."

WhatsApp our team for a Google Maps pin and we'll also help guide your driver to the resort entrance.
